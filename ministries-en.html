Ministries

There is a place for you: to serve, grow, and belong.

Join a ministry Support our mission

The Fatima Apostolate Movement is a Marian movement with a mission of reparation, adoration, and consolation of the Sacred Hearts. Our main work is to pray for the sanctification of priests, vocations, the souls in purgatory, and Mary’s intentions. We carry this out through parish activities such as the Vocational Altar (a weekly home visit of Our Blessed Mother), prayer intentions shared among families, support for children in CCD to pray for seminarians, and First Friday devotions in reparation to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. We are at your service.

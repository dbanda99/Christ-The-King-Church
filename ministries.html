The Fatima Apostles Movement is a Marian movement with a mission of reparation, adoration, and consolation to the Sacred Hearts. Our main task is to pray for the sanctification of priests, vocations, the salvation of souls in purgatory, and Mary's intentions. We accomplish this through activities within our apostolates, including: The Vocational Altar: In our parish of Christ the King, at every Mass celebrated, we have the opportunity to unite in prayer as a praying Church, receiving the visit of our Blessed Mother in homes to bless them, enthroning the Sacred Heart of Jesus as King and Lord of their homes, and consecrating all members of their families to the Sacred Hearts of Jesus, Mary, and Joseph. Each family takes on the mission of praying for one of 52 different petitions and virtues each week, thus covering our entire community with Mary's Mantle. We are grateful to the 208 families who join this prayer apostolate throughout the year. In conjunction with the CCD program, the children of the community receive a Vocational Bear to encourage them to pray for seminarians. On the First Fridays of Reparation to the Sacred Heart of Jesus, we pray for the sanctification of all our priests in our Diocese, especially for our parish priest, Father Toribio Guerrero, to whom we are grateful for his unconditional support for our Movement. We offer the Mass, and families can take the Sacred Heart Altar with the Divine Mercy novena for a sick person or a novena for a deceased family member during the month. We do everything to give glory to God and in accordance with His Divine Will. We are at your service.
